Which Should You Choose?

Both tools are excellent AI writing assistants, but they serve slightly different needs. Your choice depends on your team size, budget, and specific requirements.

🤖 Choose Jasper AI if...

You need enterprise-grade security (SOC 2), built-in SEO optimization with Surfer integration, AI image generation, and plagiarism checking. Best for larger marketing teams that need brand voice consistency at scale.

✍ Choose Copy.ai if...

You want a generous free tier to start, need more templates (90+), prefer API access on lower-tier plans, and are focused on sales copy and workflow automation. Great for small teams and freelancers.
