Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free listing? ▼
The free listing includes a basic profile with your tool's name, description, category, and up to 3 screenshots. Users can write reviews about your tool, and you'll get basic analytics showing page views.
What is a dofollow backlink and why does it matter? ▼
A dofollow backlink from InfoWebWorld Directory passes SEO authority to your website, helping improve your search engine rankings. Our domain has high authority, making these backlinks particularly valuable for your SEO strategy.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade at any time and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. No long-term contracts required.
How does the lead capture form work? ▼
On Growth plans and above, a customizable lead capture form appears on your listing page. Visitors can submit their contact info, which is delivered to you via email and available in your dashboard. Pro plans include CRM sync with HubSpot, Salesforce, and more.
What is buyer intent data? ▼
Enterprise plans include access to buyer intent signals: you can see which companies are viewing your listing, comparing you with competitors, and reading reviews. This helps your sales team prioritize outreach to warm leads.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
