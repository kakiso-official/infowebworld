Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my tool on TechRadar?

Visit our Submit Listing page to get started. We offer free and paid listing options.

How do I claim my company profile?

If your tool is already listed, click "Claim this profile" on your listing page and verify ownership via your company email.

How do I report a fake review?

Click the flag icon on any review to report it. Our moderation team reviews all flagged content within 48 hours.

Can I edit my listing after submission?

Yes, all paid plans include full editing access through the vendor dashboard. Free listings can request edits via email.
