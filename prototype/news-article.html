OpenAI Launches GPT-5 with Breakthrough Reasoning Capabilities

Source: TechCrunch March 10, 2026 · 2 hours ago 2 min read

AI Summary

OpenAI has officially launched GPT-5, its most advanced language model to date, featuring significant improvements in multi-step reasoning and drastically reduced hallucination rates. The model achieves 92% accuracy on graduate-level reasoning benchmarks, up from 78% with GPT-4o. Key innovations include native multimodal understanding, a 1M token context window, and the ability to execute multi-step tasks autonomously. Enterprise pricing starts at $0.01 per 1K tokens, making it 40% cheaper than GPT-4o at launch. The model is available immediately through the API and will roll out to ChatGPT Plus users over the next week.
